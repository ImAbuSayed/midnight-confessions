[Hook]
Midnight shadows dancing on the cabin wall
I'm answering the magic of your quiet call (quiet call)
The embers burning low and sweet and slow
I just want to be wherever you want to go

[Verse 1]
There's a gentle breeze blowing through the open door
But I only see the moonlight resting on the floor
Your silhouette is painted in the amber light
We're trading sweet secrets in the middle of the night (middle of the night)

[Pre-Chorus]
Every single breath is pulling me closer to your skin

It's a slow burn wrapped in a heavy sigh
Underneath the canvas of a starless sky

[Chorus]

Tangled in the moment, leaning in to kiss
A hundred unspoken words floating in the dark
Leaving a wild fire burning right across my heart (across my heart)

[Post-Chorus]
